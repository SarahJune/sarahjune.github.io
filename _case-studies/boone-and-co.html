Boone & Co

The Project

Boone & Co is a concept for a company that provides campground and outdoor conference registrations, to encourage and facilitate more open-air events.

My Role

For this project, I drove the ideation for the overall company branding and individual UI design, created prototypes for a sign-up page, a credit card payment screen, and an event landing page, and wrote the copy for each user interface.

Research

Research for this project involved reviewing similar user experiences for sign-in pages and payment interfaces. For the landing page, I brainstormed what someone who was signing up for a conference would need to know, such as how to prepare and what to expect.

UX Design

As I created each prototype, I looked at the structure of a simple sign-in screen, and what conventions I would need to include so that the user would be able to navigate each view. I was able to follow conventions for the basic structure, such as including the option to create an account on the sign-in page, should a user not have an account already. For the payment user interface, I included both the payment information and the cart view, so that users could review their purchase before finalizing payment.

Visual Design

The visual design was developed out of the theme of the company: inspiring people to be in the outdoors. The color palette included a lot of natural tones, while including a brighter green to use for calls to action, buttons, etc.
