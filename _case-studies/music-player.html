Boone & Co

The Project

Boone & Co is a concept for a company that provides camp ground and woodland conference registrations, to encourage and facilitate more outdoor excersions. The challenge was to create a sign-up page, a credit card payment screen, and a landing page.

My Role

I wrote the copy, established the branding, and designed the prototype for this project.
